The Method

Solve et Coagula

Every REROOT trajectory follows an alchemical architecture that the body already understands: first dissolve what's stuck, then restructure at a deeper level.

Solve — activation destabilizes existing patterns. Muscle armor, held emotion, breath restriction. The charge moves through what was frozen.

Coagula — integration restructures at a new level. Grounding, silence, conscious breath. What dissolved finds a new form.

Without Solve there is no change. Without Coagula there is no integration. A session that only activates is dangerous. A session that only grounds is empty.

This is not decorative metaphor. It is the biomechanical rhythm of bio-electric activation. The body knows this sequence — it's been doing it since before we named it.

Ancient correspondence

The Kemetic tradition called this current Sekhem — power. The Yoruba call it Àṣẹ. The Bambara call it Nyama. Three names for the same measurable phenomenon. REROOT restores the precision the Western esoteric tradition lost in translation.
