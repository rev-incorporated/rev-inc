Frequently Asked

Before you book.

Does it hurt?+

No pain — only the resistance you create yourself. The current goes through the muscles and the nervous system. If you don't resist, it's a profound pleasure. The body receives warmth, tingling, and a deep sense of saturation.

Is it safe?+

Yes. DDS microcurrent operates at voltages well below any risk threshold. Contraindications: metal implants in the treatment area, pacemakers, and pregnancy. Outside of these, the technology is completely safe.

How many sessions do I need?+

You feel results from session one — a pattern break and diagnostic reading. Session two targets specific openings. Session three builds new patterns. From three sessions, sustainable change begins. But the body starts responding immediately.

How is this different from massage, physio, or reiki?+

ZZAPP works with microvolt that goes into the body — through the muscles, speaking directly to the nervous system, charging mitochondria. It goes beyond the mechanical. Emotions and stored patterns mapped onto the body are addressed at the bio-electric level. Not purely spiritual or subtle — this is biophysics with biological functionality.

Can I combine this with therapy or medication?+

Yes. ZZAPP complements therapy, coaching, and medication — it doesn't replace them. Many clients combine bio-electric sessions with their existing care. The body's capacity to process is enhanced, which supports whatever work you're already doing.

What should I do after a session?+

Hydrate with electrolytes. Stretch. Avoid submerging in water for the first 2 hours. Ground yourself — walk barefoot, be in nature. Movement practices like yin yoga, qigong, tai chi, or capoeira amplify the integration. Focus on new things or things you want more of — you'll have the energy, and the body's reward system makes new habits easier to build.
